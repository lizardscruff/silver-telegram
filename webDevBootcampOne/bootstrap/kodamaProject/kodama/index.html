What is Hinoki?

Hinoki is Japanese for the Cypress trees that grow in the forests of rural Japan. It is traditionally used for building shrines, houses and bath tubs. It is highly regarded for its water resistance and anti-bacterial properties. These characteristics, along with itâ€™s pleasant cypress aroma, make it a perfect material to be used in the kitchen.
